About Me

I discovered my passion for coding while distance teaching my daughter this spring. I found teaching her and learning simple coding processes was something I enjoyed. I have submerged myself in the field of Software Engineering, becoming an efficient web developer.

When I am not coding, I run an independent record label called Learning Curve Records. Working with artists worldwide, I have coordinated press campaigns, tours, and networking for clients.

In my spare time, I love to spend time with my daughter Iris. Gardening in our yard, building projects, having fun are top activities for the two of us. I also enjoy cooking and listening to vinyl records.
